FAQ — Strategic Presence

What is it?

A paid presence to obtain clarity and decision with no method or promise.

Threshold?

3,690 CHF for a 60–90 minute individual session. Board sessions are priced higher.

Deliverable?

No imposed deliverable. Sometimes a sentence. Sometimes a pivot. Always a decision.

For whom?

Decision-makers and individuals willing to pay for useful friction and the absence of promise.

What about individuals?

The threshold is the same. 60–90 minutes. If you tried coaches, therapists, or healers without result, this presence is for you.
